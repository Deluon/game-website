F.A.Q.

We know you all have questions, and here is where we will answer the majority of them. These are just a few of the questions we get asked most, and if you need one answered that isn't here, feel free to contact us with your question and we will get back to you ASAP. Meanwhile, hope you find your answers here.

What can I buy now? Are pinball or arcade games available for purchase?

You can buy almost any. Arcade games, both cabinet and pinball are purchasable if you know where to find them. Ebay is a good source for people to start looking, and there are probably some companies that refurbish decommissioned ones for home use and sell them at a profit.

Can I play my old games on new systems? For example, Temujin was for Windows 95. Can I play that on Windows 7 or any newer OS

Absolutely. In my experience computers tend to be backwards compatible. In the end, newer models still function exactly the same at the base levels, the only thing that changed was the hardware and how much power they have.
