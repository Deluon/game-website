PlayStation

The PlayStation was the first console of it's kind to take the market the way it did. The graphics were top of the line and the games were relatively cheap to produce, allowing a greater profit margin for Sony. Some of my favorite games for it were Spyro the Dragon, Crash Bandicoot, and Tomb Raider.

Nintendo

Nintendo is a company of many accomplishments, creating hand crafted playing cards when it first started before moving on to mechanical toys. It gained real popularity with the release of it's first major selling console, the NES. Nintendo went on to create the SNES which had improved graphics, but still relied on cartridges before creating the most popular hand held system on the market: the GameBoy. Nintendo created it's last cartridge compatible console, the Nintendo 64 shortly after the release of the PlayStation and today continues to innovate the world of gaming by being the first to successfully create a motion-detection console, which has inspired systems like the Kinect and PlayStation Move. Some of the more popular games thought up by this company are Pokemon, Legend of Zelda, and Mario.
