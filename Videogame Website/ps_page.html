PlayStation

Nintendo was founded as a card company back in 1889 before the limitations of being a playing card company were met. Nintendo went into toy development in the 1960's and then began selling video game consoles in the 1970s. Today it is still a gaming powerhouse and renowned the world over for it's classic video gaming characters and nostalgic gameplay. They created the NES, SNES, and Nintendo 64 before making consoles you all know today like the Wii and Nintendo 3DS.

The Prototype to the PSNES was the first attempt to make a Nintendo system with a disk drive. At this time, the disk drive was just an add-on.

The PSNES was a successful merging of the Nintendo cartridge and Sony's CD technology. This was the predecessor to the PSX or PlayStation X

The PlayStation was the first console to use solely CD technology, outselling other consoles with stunning 3d graphics and state of the art cut scenes.

Spyro the Dragon is a third person fantasy adventure game and the first I ever played for PlayStation. While still popular, it is no Pokemon or Mario.

Tomb Raider is an action adventure game where the objective is to collect an artifact by guiding the main character, Laura Croft through a series of tombs.

Crash Bandicoot is a fantasy platform game created by Naughty Dog. Like the other two games listed, it is third person.
