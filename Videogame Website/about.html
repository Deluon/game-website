About Us

  We are an online museum dedicated to bringing classic video games and their origins back out into the light. We look over some of the brief histories of some of gaming's biggest names such as Nintendo, PlayStation, and more as soon as we add them. We also cover a bit about classic video games and their origins, sometimes even talking about how they relate to modern day video games if a link is there to be made.

  We started in 2014 in order to give the world a better understanding of one of today's most popular sources of entertainment, and we hope to be around for many years to come bringing more information to you. We here at Retro-Spective hope to share our love of gaming and the roots of our favorite games with you. We are also open to information donations. Donate us information with any sources you used to collect it, and we will put you in the references under "Donors".
