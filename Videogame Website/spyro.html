Spyro the Dragon

  Spyro the Dragon was released in 1998 by Insomniac Games. It is a third person fantasy adventure game where the player takes the role of a dragon named Spyro. The quest is to release all of the elder dragons, collect all of the gems in all of the worlds, and defeat the main antagonist Gnasty Gnork. Spyro is kept alive by his dragonfly, Sparx who goes through three stages of health: Yellow (full), blue (mid-health), and green (low health) before vanishing altogether, leaving Spyro just one more hit before losing a life. Upon completing 100% of the game, the player can access the last hidden level, Gnasty's Loot which has a special surprise that I won't spoil here.
