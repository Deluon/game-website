Tomb Raider

  Tomb Raider is a third person action adventure game where the player takes on the role of the archaeologist, Laura Croft. As Laura you must make your way through various tombs (hence the name "Tomb Raider") in search of an artefact called The Scion, which comes in three pieces. With each piece, Laura receives visions about where the next piece is located and more information on her employer (for this game), Jacqueline Natla, owner of Natla Technologies. Again we won't spoil too much about this to encourage people to find out for themselves, preferably by playing it.
